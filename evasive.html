About the project

Evasive maneuvers an asymmetrical online multiplayer game in which you play as either runners or hunters.

Runners scour the map for energy to power up their escape portal while hunters try to catch and destroy them. Runners and hunters each have different abilities they select.

As well, players can visually customize their hunter and runner ships, choosing models, patterns, and colours.
